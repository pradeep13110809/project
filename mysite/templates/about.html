about Us

Our trained technicians at Vehicle Garage Service, can service any vehicles. We drive on the know-how and technology of the Vehicle Garage network to offer you excellent performance at a fair price.

Vehicles Garage Service is one of the world's largest independent workshop chains. Our international service network encompasses more than 15,000 authorized workshops in 150 countries.With our wide range of expert services, we can offer everything you could possibly need for your bike and car service - from inspection, periodic maintenance, accidental repairs, replacement of genuine auto parts, value added services that puts and extra smile on your face.In India, we have over 270 Vehicles Garage Service workshops, so you are sure to find one local to you who will be happy to assist you.

When you visit a Vehicles Garage Service you can depend on our expertise to get the job done. Regardless of how well you know your car, you can always feel confident in the advice you are given and the standard of workmanship you will receive. That’s why Vehicles Garage have produced this Code of Practice for our approved network of garages.

Our exceptional quality can be defined as the sum total of first class products, professional service and the satisfaction of customer expectations. Our Vehicles Garage Service workshops make their customers welcome, do a professional job and have the vehicle ready for collection on time.

Defined standards and established processes help to ensure we can always offer the best solution. Regular quality checks make sure our standards are maintained. These form the basis for our efforts to carry on improving – with particular emphasis on training and qualifications.
